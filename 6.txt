Static Single Vehicle Pickup and Delivery Problem:

	Objective :
	Objective is to minimize a weighted combination of the time  needed to serve all clients and total degree of dissatisfaction clients experience until their		 delivering.
	i.e -     Min(w1*T +  w2* (sum (wi*a+(2-a)*Ri)) )

	T - Route length 
	Wi - Waiting for client i 
	Ri - Riding time of client i from  pickup to delivery
